Digital Electronics and Computer Architecture

Digital Electronics and Computer Architecture (DECA) focuses on the design and functioning of digital circuits and computing systems. It covers topics like logic gates, microprocessors, memory architecture, and data processing. Understanding DECA is essential for building efficient hardware and optimizing software performance.
